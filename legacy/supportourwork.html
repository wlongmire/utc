Volunteering

Urban Tree Connection is a grassroots organization, and we rely on the partnership of community volunteers to make our work possible. Last year, 978 volunteers contributed more than 7,000 hours of their time at our farms and garden sites. We appreciate our volunteers from Haddington and across the city and region that have become partners, and their significant contributions over the growing seasons!

Apprenticeships/Internships

Farming Apprenticeship

Farm Apprentices will receive 1:1 training with our farm staff in sustainable vegetable production along with a share in the harvest. Apprenticeships can be structured for 3 to 9 months during the growing season.

Grants & Communications Internship

UTC is currently seeking an intern that can support with grant writing, communications, and social media. We are seeking 6 month to year-long interns.

For more information or to apply, please email us at: noelle@urbantreeconnection.org.
